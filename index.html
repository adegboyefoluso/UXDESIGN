Pets are nature's gift to humanity. It has been scientifically proven that opening our homes and hearts to a pet increases our longevity and improves our overall quality of life as well as the lives of our pets. At Pet Paradigm Professionals, our mission is to offer resources to help you care for your furry, scaly, feathery, and slimy loved ones. Our pet experts—or “Pexperts”—have been working with pet owners and professionals alike for the past twelve years. They offer one-on-one consultations with current and prospective pet owners as well as group presentations designed for veterinary, pet shelter, and pet breeding professionals.

Looking for basic pet care advice for the most common type of pets? Need additional help determining which type of pet is right for you and your family? We will work with you and provide tailored evidence-based pet care to ensure lifelong health and wellness of your new companion.

About Us

With over a decade of experience in working closely with pet owners, Paradigm Pet Professionals brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table. Our 12 years of hands-on experience have allowed us to develop a deep understanding of the various needs and challenges faced by pet owners.

Paradigm Pet Professionals goes above and beyond by holding certifications in pet health and nutrition. These certifications reflect our commitment to remaining current with the latest research and industry best practices in order to provide top-notch care for pets. By actively seeking specialized knowledge in pet health and nutrition, we are well-prepared to offer well-informed advice and effective solutions to enhance the overall well-being of beloved furry companions. This dedication to staying abreast of developments in the field ensures that pet owners can rely on Paradigm Pet Professionals to provide informed guidance and tailored recommendations for the optimal health and nutrition of their pets..

Services

Paradigm Pet Professionals offers a range of services designed to cater to the specific needs of pet owners. We provide convenient virtual consultations and presentations, allowing pet owners to seek expert advice from the comfort of their own homes. Whether it's discussing general pet care topics or addressing specific concerns about individual pets, We offer one-on-one consultations to provide personalized guidance and support. For groups interested in learning about pet care, Paradigm Pet Professionals also offers group presentations that can be tailored to meet the unique needs and interests of the audience. These presentations cover a wide range of general pet care topics, providing valuable insights and practical tips to always ensure the well-being of furry companions.
